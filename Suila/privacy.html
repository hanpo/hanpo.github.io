Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 19, 2026

Suila（水啦）("the App") is a free video editor developed in Taiwan by an independent developer ("the Developer"). This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your information.

In short: Suila collects nothing. There are no accounts, no ads, no analytics, and no tracking. Your videos, audio, and edits stay on your device.

Information We Collect

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. There is no account system, no login, and no user registration. The Developer has no servers that receive your data.

Photos & Media

With your permission, the App imports videos and music that you select so you can edit them. All media and project files are stored locally on your device. Nothing is uploaded. Exported videos are saved back to your photo library only when you choose to export, and only with your permission.

Speech Recognition (Auto Captions)

The automatic caption feature transcribes the audio of the videos you are editing. On iOS 26, this transcription runs entirely on your device using Apple's on-device speech framework. Your audio is not sent to the Developer or to any third party.

Advertising & Analytics

The App contains no advertising, no analytics SDKs, and no third-party tracking. The App does not use Apple's Advertising Identifier (IDFA) and does not present an App Tracking Transparency prompt.

In-App Purchases

The App is completely free and contains no in-app purchases and no subscriptions.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party advertising, analytics, or data-collection services. Standard Apple frameworks (such as Photos and on-device Speech) are used solely to provide the App's features and do not transmit your data to the Developer.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. Because the App collects no personal information from anyone, it does not knowingly collect information from children.

Data Retention

All media, projects, and settings are stored locally on your device. The Developer has no access to this data. Uninstalling the App removes all locally stored data.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please submit a support request.
