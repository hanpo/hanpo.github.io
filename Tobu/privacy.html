Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 16, 2026

Tobu ("the App") is developed by an independent developer ("the Developer"). This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your information.

Collection of Personal Information

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. There is no account system, no login, and no user registration.

Camera, Microphone & Photos

The App uses your device's camera and microphone to record video clips. All recordings are stored locally on your device and are never uploaded to any server. The App may save videos to your photo library with your permission.

Advertising

The App uses Google AdMob to display banner and interstitial advertisements. AdMob may collect the following data:

  • Device ID (Advertising Identifier / IDFA) — used to serve personalized ads
  • Usage data — app interactions, ad views, and clicks
  • Crash data — diagnostic information for stability improvements

This data is collected by Google and is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

App Tracking Transparency

The App requests your permission via Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework before allowing AdMob to use your Advertising Identifier for personalized ads. You may deny this request and still use the App normally.

In-App Purchases

The App offers a one-time "Remove Ads" purchase. Purchase transactions are processed entirely by Apple and are subject to Apple's Privacy Policy. The Developer does not have access to your payment information.

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob — advertising (Privacy Policy)
  • Apple StoreKit — in-app purchases

The Developer does not share your personal information with any third parties.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. The Developer does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Data Retention

All video recordings and app settings are stored locally on your device. The Developer has no access to this data. Uninstalling the App will remove all locally stored data.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Disclaimer

The Developer assumes no responsibility for any theft, damage, or any other problems that may occur while using this App. Please be aware of this.

Contact

If you have any questions, please submit a support request.
